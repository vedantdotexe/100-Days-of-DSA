### 🟢 Day 25: April 3, 2026
**Topic:** Search Insert Position
**Language:** Java ☕
**Vibe:** 🎯 Precision Searching

Today I worked on a variation of Binary Search. I learned that Binary Search isn't just for finding a value; it's also for finding the correct "boundary." Even when a target isn't in the array, the `left` pointer ends up at the index where the value belongs. This is super useful for maintaining sorted data.
