# 🏁 100 Days of DSA: The Strategy 🔵🔴🟡

> **"Programs must be written for people to read, and only incidentally for machines to execute."** — Harold Abelson

This repository tracks my daily progress from absolute zero to advanced DSA. My goal is to understand the "Why" behind the code, focusing on high-performance logic for MERN and AI applications.

---

## 🏎️ The Season Roadmap (Revised)

### 🛠️ Phase 1: The Power Unit (Days 1–25)
*Focus: Mastering Arrays & Logic from Scratch*
- [ ] **Week 1:** Array Fundamentals (Search, Memory, Min/Max, Swapping).
- [ ] **Week 2:** The Efficiency Shift (Binary Search & Sorted Arrays).
- [ ] **Week 3:** String Manipulation & Two-Pointer patterns.
- [ ] **Week 4:** Linked Lists & Basic Memory Management.

### 🧩 Phase 2: The Mid-Field Battle (Days 26–50)
*Focus: Logic & Recursion*
- [ ] Stacks & Queues (LIFO/FIFO).
- [ ] Hashing & Objects for $O(1)$ lookup.
- [ ] Recursion (Introduction to "Divide and Conquer").

### 🏁 Phase 3 & 4: High Downforce & The Final Lap (Days 51–100)
*Focus: Advanced Structures & Interview Prep*
- [ ] Trees, Graphs, and Dynamic Programming (DP).
