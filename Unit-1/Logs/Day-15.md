### 🟢 Day 15: March 12, 2026
**Topic:** Optimized Sliding Window (Sliding Window Algorithm)
**Vibe:** 🏁 Unit 1 Complete!

Today was the final day of the Array unit. I upgraded my "Sliding Frame" logic from yesterday. Instead of re-calculating the sum from scratch every time the window moves, I learned to just subtract the element leaving the window and add the one entering it.

The "Aha!" moment was realizing how much work the computer saves by not repeating the same additions. This optimization took my code from O(n*k) to O(n), which is huge for large datasets. I’m feeling much more confident about how memory and time work together. Unit 1 is officially in the books!
