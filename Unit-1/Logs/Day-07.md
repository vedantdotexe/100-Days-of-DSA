### 🟢 Day 7: March 4, 2026
**Topic:** Rotating by K (The Reversal Algorithm)
**Vibe:** 🏆 One Week Strong!

I’ve officially completed 7 days of DSA! Today I leveled up from rotating by 1 to rotating by K. Instead of just looping the Day 6 logic multiple times, I learned the "Triple Reversal" trick. 

The "Aha!" moment was seeing how reversing sections of the array actually "pushes" them to the back in the correct order. It felt like a puzzle coming together. By using three targeted reversals, I kept the time complexity at O(n) instead of O(n*k). It’s the most efficient way to handle shifts in data, and a great way to close out my first week of the challenge.
